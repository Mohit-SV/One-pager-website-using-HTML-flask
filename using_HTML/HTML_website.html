About us


Analyze the tunneling current through molecules in various media both at normal and cryogenic conditions using edges of two twisted graphene layers as the electrodes to study the behavior of electron transport in single molecules.The aim is to achieve high precision real-time characterization of the molecules.

The project involves building as- semblies (preparation of graphene, masking it to avoid current leakage; modifying STM and creating sample holders to work at different conditions and media) and character- izing them to fabricate the best design of the setup, experimenting using thus created setup. The project helps in the development of molecular electronics, bio-sensing and a method for DNA sequencing.
